How to do smart SEO?

Search engine optimization is not about putting in keywords, it is really about understanding the data we have. We use tools like Google Analytics and Google Search Console to get a report on what users are doing on our website right now where our traffic is coming from and how our links are being indexed. Search engine optimization is about looking at this information to make decisions.

Why continuous optimization is a must?

The world of the internet and search engine algorithms change every day. A strategy that works today might not work tomorrow. Therefore one should constantly monitor a website's performance, a websites loading speed and a website's mobile responsiveness.Conducting audits helps both beginners and professionals identify a website's shortcomings thereby enabling us to keep the website consistently fast and user-friendly.
